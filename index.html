Safe Additional Snacks

  • Apart from her 2 main feeds, only give low-calorie foods like boiled carrots.
  • You can also use small amounts of other approved boiled and cooled vegetables as frequent treats.
  • A single large treat is permitted once a day.

Key Guidelines

  • Preparation: Always pre-soak kibble in water.
  • Preparation: Boil and cool any added vegetables.
  • Strictly Prohibited: No mushrooms, onions, garlic, avocado, or general human food.

‚ö†Ô∏è High Reactivity Warning

She is highly reactive to other dogs and pets.

  • Must be kept on a short leash in the presence of other dogs.
  • She has a tendency to lunge, even if her tail is wagging.
  • Be extremely vigilant for dogs around corners.
  • Leash Safety: Must be ON LEASH at all times outdoors; never let her off-leash.
  • Leash Safety: Use the provided waist leash and wrap the lead securely around your hand.
  • Distractions: Do not use your phone while walking; focus entirely on her surroundings.
  • Street Items: Prevent her from eating any food or items found on the street.

Stomach & Health Support

  • Zoolac Probiotic: Give 1 small dose daily to keep her stomach healthy while she is on the steroid medication.
  • Thirst: Prednisolone causes extreme thirst; ensure constant access to fresh water.
  • Shivering: Shivering or trembling is a common side effect.

General Information

üè† Critical Dog-Proofing

If you have to leave her alone, you must dog-proof the house before leaving:

  • Keep all food items strictly out of reach.
  • Store anything aromatic (fragrant items) out of reach.
  • Keep the **bathroom door closed** to keep soaps and candles out of reach.
  • Secure all plastics and reachable blinds.

Home Alone Protocol

  • Give one treat and make eye contact before leaving.
  • Always say "be good" upon departure.
