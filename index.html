Key Feeding Guidelines:

On-Leash Safety and Reactivity Management

  • Leash Only: The dog must be ON LEASH at all times outdoors. Never let her off-leash.
  • Focus: Do not be distracted (e.g., by your phone) while walking.
  • **Leash Security:** Use the provided waist leash *and* wrap the leash securely around your hand.
  • **Reactivity:** She will bark and may lunge at other dogs. Be vigilant for dogs around corners.
  • **Street Items:** Watch out for and prevent her from eating food found on the street.
  • **Weather:** Be firm if she is reluctant to walk in cold or rain, as she must go out for the minimum required time.
